What is httpet?

httpet serves friendly, themed images for HTTP status codes.

Each pet gets its own subdomain and image set.

How to use it

Pick a pet and a status code:

Subdomain: {{ pet_example_url }}/404

Path: {{ frontend_url }}/dog/404

Use the homepage to see available pets and status codes.

Vote for new pets

Have a pet you want to see? Vote for it on the homepage.

Votes are tallied daily to decide what gets added next.
